Sofi Vesta

Starting as a violinist in St.Petersburg, Russia, she soon gravitated to the city's underground clubs and forest raves, where her passion for electronic music ignited. After moving to China, she honed a signature sound blending tribal and ethnic house before mastering her sound in beach club residencies in Thailand.
